# Serafina UW Phoenix AZ - Table Inventory

## Overview
This document catalogs all tables found in each sheet of the underwriting model.  
**Total Sheets:** 12

---

## 1. Property Summary
**Tables: 4**

1. **Property Description** - Basic property info (name, address, city, state, county, zip, land area, year built, management type/company)
2. **Property Size** - Physical characteristics (total units, avg unit size, current vacancy, buildings, floors)
3. **Purchase Metrics** - Financial acquisition data (T12 revenue/expenses/NOI, guidance price, implied cap rate, price per unit, call for offers date)
4. **Seller Information** - Seller details (name, ownership duration, last sold price, selling motivation, improvements made, priorities, broker contact info)

---

## 2. Demos
**Tables: 8**

1. **CoStar Properties: Demographics** - Population, growth, median HH income, median home value, renter % (1/3/5 mile radius)
2. **Best Places** - Crime rates (violent & property) for zip code vs national average
3. **Great Schools** - School ratings (elementary & high school)
4. **CoStar Properties: Summary** - Walk score, transit score
5. **CoStar Properties: Analytics - Submarket** - Submarket vacancy, inventory units, delivered units, construction %, cap rate, sale price/unit
6. **CoStar Properties: Analytics - Market** - Market-level vacancy, inventory, delivered units, construction %, cap rate, sale price/unit
7. **Property Rating Scorecard** - Weighted scoring matrix for: population, population growth, median HH income, median home value, renter %, crime rates, school ratings, walk/transit scores, submarket vacancy, delivered/construction % of inventory
8. **Property Rating Decision** - Decision thresholds (Move Forward ≥7.0, Quick Check with Team ≥5.5, Don't Move Forward <5.5)

---

## 3. Historical Financials
**Tables: 4**

1. **Property Units** - Total units, occupied units, occupancy %
2. **Rental Income** - Potential rental income, loss to lease, vacancy loss, bad debt, concessions, total rental income, RUBS income, other income, total revenue (T12/T6/T3 with per unit calculations)
3. **Operating Expenses** - G&A, payroll, leasing & marketing, utilities, repairs & maintenance, HOA, turnover, contract services, insurance, property taxes, management fees, total OpEx, expense ratio
4. **Net Operating Income** - NOI calculation (T12/T6/T3 with per unit)

---

## 4. Unit Mix & Comps
**Tables: 5**

1. **Property Unit Mix** - Detailed unit breakdown by type/SF (units, occupied, renovation units, unit mix %, advertised rent, gap to achieved, achieved rent, price per SF)
2. **Market Rent Comparison** - Market average asking rent, market price per SF, current vs market price per SF delta
3. **Renovated Rent Premiums** - Market high asking rent, renovated rent, rent premium, premium %
4. **Unit Type Legend** - Classic, Prior Upgrade (PU), Upgrade (U), Elite (E) definitions
5. **Summary Statistics** - Total/average calculations for SF, units, occupancy, renovation counts

---

## 5. Revenue
**Tables: 7**

1. **Anticipated RUBS Income** - Water/sewer, trash, pest control, package hub, valet trash per unit assumptions
2. **Unit Renovations** - Renovation start month, units per month, total renovation units, unit type breakdown with current rent and rent premiums
3. **Anticipated Other Income** - T12 other income vs Year 1 proforma comparison
4. **Gross Potential Rents** - Current rent, market rent, renovated rent premium (monthly/annual/avg per unit, above/below market)
5. **Income Growth Assumptions** - 10-year projections for RUBS growth, other income growth, rental income growth, monthly rent projections, vacancy/bad debt/concessions assumptions
6. **Projected Annual Revenue** - 10-year annual projections for: year end units, offline units, renovated units, occupancy %, loss to lease %, all revenue line items
7. **Monthly Revenue Projections** - Detailed month-by-month revenue projections (analysis year, date, units, occupancy, all revenue line items)

---

## 6. Expenses
**Tables: 5**

1. **Per Unit Analysis** - T12 expenses vs Year 1 proforma by category (G&A, payroll, leasing, utilities, repairs, HOA, turnover, contract services, insurance, property taxes, management fees)
2. **Payroll Assumptions** - Detailed payroll breakdown by position (manager, asst manager, maintenance, tech) with hourly rates, wages, benefits, totals
3. **Property Tax Assessment** - Purchase price, assessed value %, tax rate, assessed property tax, manual override, reassessment years
4. **Expense Growth Assumptions** - Annual growth rates by category (insurance, payroll, R&M, tax reassessment, tax growth, other expenses, management fee %)
5. **Projected Annual Expenses** - 10-year expense projections by category with expense ratio and reassessment year flags

## 7. CapEx
**Tables: 4**

1. **Unit Renovations** - By unit type: available units, renovation units, cost per unit, total cost, rent premium, renovation ROI
2. **Renovation Cost Breakdown (Standard)** - Itemized costs: appliances, vinyl flooring, hardware/fixtures, cabinet fronts, backsplash, counters, paint, lighting, WD connections/units, bathroom, smart home, labor, other
3. **General Improvements** - Line items: exterior wood repairs, package hub, asphalt, dog park, lighting, security cameras, paint exterior, signage, landscaping, concrete, leasing office, mailboxes, fitness center, gym flooring, laundry room, key fob, misc
4. **Sourcing From China** - Alternative cost breakdown for materials sourced from China

## 8. Project Budget
**Tables: 5**

2. **CapEx Budget** - Immediate repairs, unit renovations, general improvements, contingency, working capital reserve
3. **Financing Costs** - Loan origination, lender legal, Phase I/appraisal/property condition, mortgage tax, broker fee, rate lock
4. **Wind River Group Fees** - Acquisition fee, CapEx management fee, total project cost summary
5. **Monthly Budget Schedule** - Month-by-month budget drawdown schedule

## 9. Capital Stack

1. **Loan Information** - Acquisition loan vs refinance loan comparison: term, rate index, current rate, rate type, spread, interest rate, IO period, amortization, LTV, minimum DSCR, loan amount, DSCR calculation
2. **Current Treasuries** - Treasury rates by term (3, 5, 7, 10 year)
3. **Sources & Uses** - Sources: senior loan, mezzanine, LP equity, GP equity / Uses: purchase price, acquisition costs, CapEx budget, financing costs, acquisition fee, CapEx management fee, lender reserves (with amounts and %)

## 10. Cash Flows
**Tables: 6**

1. **Property Summary** - Year end units, offline units, renovated units, occupancy %, loss to lease % (T12 + 10-year projections)
2. **Total Revenue** - 10-year projections: potential rental income, loss to lease, vacancy, offline renovation loss, bad debt, concessions, rental income, RUBS, other income, total revenue
3. **Operating Expenses** - 10-year projections: all expense categories, expense ratio
4. **Net Operating Income & Cash Flow** - NOI, asset management fees, replacement reserves, operating cash flow, debt service (interest/principal), lender reserves, net cash flow
5. **Annual Financial Metrics** - Cash on cash, yield on cost, debt yield, DSCR (years 1-7)
6. **Monthly Cash Flows** - Detailed month-by-month: analysis year, project month, date, units, occupancy, all revenue/expense line items, NOI, asset management fees, reserves, operating cash flow, debt service schedule

## 11. Waterfall
**Tables: 8**

1. **Equity & Waterfall Structure** - Total equity invested, LP/GP equity %, preferred return, hurdle rate, share splits by tier
2. **Prospective Investor Investment** - Sample investor amount
3. **Disposition Assumptions** - Month, exit cap rate, gross sales price, selling costs, debt repayment, proceeds to equity
4. **Selling Costs** - Closing costs, broker fee, disposition fee percentages
5. **Total Limited Partner(s) Return** - 10-year: capital investment, preferred return, tier 1 split, tier 2 split, total return, IRR, equity multiple
6. **Total General Partner(s) Return** - Same structure as LP
7. **Project Level Returns** - Same structure, plus total capital invested and total return
8. **Monthly Waterfall Details** - Month-by-month: NOI, net cash flow by LP/GP, refinance/disposition proceeds, IRR waterfall mechanics (beginning balance, capital investment, accrued pref/hurdle, payments, ending balance), tier 1 & tier 2 cash flows

## 12. Investment Summary
**Tables: 11**

1. **Property Description** - Property name, address, city, state, county, zip, land area, year built, management type/company
2. **Property Size** - Total units, total SF, avg SF per unit, current occupancy, buildings, floors
3. **Purchase Metrics** - T12 revenue/expenses/NOI, purchase price, implied cap rate, submarket cap rate, market cap rate

6. **Proposed Waterfall** - LP equity contribution %, preferred return, hurdle rate, LP share below/above hurdle
7. **Investment Returns** - By LP and project level: total equity, total return, equity multiple, IRR, avg cash on cash; price per unit comparison (purchase vs sale vs submarket vs market)
8. **Annual Financial Metrics** - Years 1-6: cash on cash, yield on cost, debt yield, DSCR
9. **Total Investment Rating Scorecard** - Property rating, year 1 cash on cash, avg cash on cash, investor IRR, investor equity multiple, investment committee score (with figures, scores, weightings, increments, and scale)
10. **Investment Decision** - Decision thresholds (Submit LOI ≥7.0, Take a Deeper Dive ≥6.0, Don't Submit LOI <6.0)
11. **Annual Cash Flows** - T12 + Years 1-6: all revenue line items, all expense line items, NOI, asset management fees, replacement reserves, operating cash flow, debt service, net cash flow
